New trends in solid particulates monitoring by ECM

ECM has come out on the market with unique electrodynamic monitors for solid particulates determination on the basis of unique electrodynamic technology and optical dust monitors working on the principle of dynamic opacity - scintillation.

Technology principle

This technology has been developed by British company PCME. It is based on the well known principle of direct voltage induction on the "electrode" by means of charges carried by solid particulates. Contribution of electrodynamic technology lays most of all in new way of signal processing.

Classic triboelectric instruments monitor voltage on the electrode created by electrical charges. These instruments are sensitive to moisture, electrode sediments and other influences do not allowing more accurate measurement. With difference to triboelectric instruments, electrodynamic methode is based on noise analysis of alternating signal component on the electrode. Alternating component is produced by charges of particulates colliding with or passing by the electrode. In such a way analyzed noise spectrum on the electrode is independent of virtually all real disturbing influences and therefore allows very accurate measurement. Shape of electrode does not have to be classic rode, there are electrodes in the shape of short stud or ring electrodes built into piping. Instruments have linear response from extremely low up to extremely high particulates concentrations.

Electrodynamic instruments series begins with the simplest types designed for filters control monitoring and continues with more complicated and accurate instruments suitable for emission applications.

DS5 / DS10

represent simple detectors for broken bag monitoring. Instruments are equipped with two alarm levels - higher level of particulates (warning) and broken bag.

DS30

is also the detector designed for broken bag monitoring. These instruments include, however, the whole series of user selectable functions and are also equipped with alarm output.

DA50

is designed for trends monitoring in emission applications and filters efficiency monitoring. User can type in as a reference level any of particulates concentrations in preselected reference time period, e.g. after a certain number of filter cleaning cycles.

Emission values of solid particulates coming from the probe are in microprocessor processed and compared with input reference level and evaluated as an emission factor. Instrument allows to input variable time constants for signal filtration and attainment of filter activity trends.

Alarm is activated, when emission factor reaches a preset value. User can also input a time constant of signal delay in order to avoid false alarms in filter cleaning cycles.

Instrument processor allows for linear signal evaluation. By means of gravimetric measurement it is possible to obtain for certain types of dust load calculation constants and to use instrument also as a portable dust monitor. Instrument is equipped with a standard 4-20 mA output.

DA50 is designed for indicative monitoring of bag, ceramic and cyclon filters in:

  • chemical industry
  • industry of iron and non-iron metals
  • galvanizing processes
  • pharmaceutical industry
  • production of rubber mixtures
  • wood industry

Approved solid particulates monitor DT770 / DT270

according to TA LUFT it is designed for continuous monitoring of solid particulates emissions. It is controlled by microprocessor and contains built-in dataloggeer for data storage. Instrument allows quantitative monitoring in mg/m3 or indicative monitoring for trends determination with data archiving. One control unit allows for data processing coming from four sensors.

It can work in the following modes:

1. Filters monitoring mode

  • graphic presentation of filter cleaning cycles

2. EPA mode

  • calculation and storage of emission averages in mg/m3 for the period of 24 hours (30 s averages) or 600 days (1 hour averages)
  • activation of alarms for immediate and average emission limits, sensor failures and memory overload
  • possibility to sent measured data to PC. EPA Reporter software creates graphic presentations corresponding with solid particulates emission monitoring requirements

3. Process control mode

  • datalogging of immediate emission values for the period of last 24 hours; hourly emission trends can be visualized on the display
  • possibility to analyse filter cleaning cycles in order to predict filter emission problems
  • possibility of connection to PC
  • instrument is extremely sensitive with dynamic range from 0.02 mg/m3 up to 1000 mg/m3
  • particulates detection from 0.1 m m
  • built-in automatic "zero" and "span" control
  • operating temperatures: 25°C to 750°C (higher temperatures also available)

SC600 - optical scintillation dust monitor

Solid particulates analyzer reminding with its construction of classic opacimeters. It consists of optical source, focussing optics and detector. However, instead of energy reduction due to presence of particulates scintillation is monitored - noise spectrum caused by particulates flying through an optical beam. This instrument allows substantially more accurate and stable measurement than it is the case with classic optical instruments.

  • instrument works on dynamic opacity - scintillation measurement principle
  • measures light change intensity (dynamic opacity) while solid particulates pass through the optical beam
  • scintillation technology allows considerable reliability and sensitivity increase in comparison with traditional opacimeters
  • measurement is independent of size, colour and surface reflexion characteristic of particulates
  • occasional optics contamination has no influence on instrument accuracy
  • flue gas darkness has no influence on measurement
  • instrument is designed for solid particulates emission monitoring, while it allows for effective and reliable usage in large size ducts, processes and applications with variable flow velocity
  • scope of application: concentration from 2 mg/m3 up to 1000 mg/m3, duct diameter 0.3 - 10 m, temperature from 25°C up to 600°C, sensitivity (at 25°C) 0.2 mg/m3
  • typical applications: energetics, waste incinerators, cement ovens and large size ducts, electrostatic filters, filtration units
  • instrument has built-in memory (16.000 measured data) or 24-hour floating memory (30 s averages)
  • calibration - by comparison with gravimetric measurement

Analyses in cement industry

Sample intake and conditioning in applications with extremely high temperature and dust load

In many applications hot gases with high dust load monitoring is required. Typical applications are cement rotary kilns, magnesium plants, metallurgy of iron and non-iron metals.

Temperatures can reach up to 1400°C and dust particulates concentration can be in the range of hundreds of grams per cubic meter. Mechanically cleaned, automatically controlled cooled probes are usually used for these applications. However, despite high price they offer only limited reliability. Customers usually fight with permanent problems with probe and filters clogging.

ECM offers to its customers elegant solution. Special, water cooled probe with the length up to 4 m, resistant to temperatures up to 1400°C is installed directly on the sampling site. Sample is drawn to the probe via fine orifice created by water spraying. Water spraying is caused by a small orifice mounted on the probe inlet. Due to water spraying the sample is drawn into the probe in the form of gas bubbles together with water mixed with solid particulates. Stream movement is provided by water ejector pump, where the sample flows through two filter assemblies for elimination of solid particulates from water mixture. Sample bubbler serves for corrosive componets elimination from the sample before analysis. Gas/water separation in the sample is provided by a condensation dryer. Dry gaseous sample is after filtration analyzed by conventional analyzers. Effective probe back purge cleaning system allows for attendance free monitoring system operation.

Safe operation even under extreme conditions as well as attractive price certainly pleasently surprise customers having experience with complicated and costly systems for these applications. Reliability of this solution has been proved by more than 50 reference installations all over the Europe.
