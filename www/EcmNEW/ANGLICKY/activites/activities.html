Main activities

Holding association ECM has been founded by Environmental Systems Corporation (ESC), US market leader with a 30 year history in the field of environmental monitoring technologies for European and Asian operations.

Established in 1991, ECM operates its subsidiaries in Slovakia, Czech Republic, Hungary, Poland, Romania and Russia. ESC directly operates subsidiaries in Thailand and China. In frame of exclusive partnership with leading manufacturers of monitoring instruments in addition ECM offers its clients high quality solutions in the area of gas and liquid composition monitoring equipment for both environmental and process applications.

Main activities ECM are :

Stack gas monitoring systems

Ambient air monitoring systems

Water quality monitoring systems

Process gas analysis

Instrument technique for safety, comfort and health

Instrument technique to enhance productivity and quality

Manufacture of isothermic shelters

Stack gas monitoring systems

ECM offers turnkey emission monitoring installations.

The following pollutants can be monitored using ECM supplied equipment :

SO2 H2S  CO NH3
NOX TRS CO2 HCl
CxHy HF Dust particles

Instrumentation for a wide range of other pollutants is available as well. Stack monitoring systems use to be completed with O2, particulate, flow, temperature and pressure measurements.

Architecture of the systems is designed to meet the highest requirements for precision, low interferences and high reliability.

An extraordinary emphasis is put on processing and presentation of data in accordance with local legislation.

Ambient air monitoring systems

ECM delivers mobile and stationary imission systems corresponding to international standards.

The following set of pollutants can be monitored with ECM supplied analysers :

SO2 NH3  TRS Heavy metals
NOX CxHy H2S Dust
CO PAH O3 VOC

Data from analysers are processed and usually stored on the measuring site. These data are transmitted to the central station via telephone line, GSM communication network or radio transmission. Imission monitoring systems can be completed with sampling equipment, portable instruments and detectors monitoring escape of dangerous substances.

Process gas measurement

ECM delivers monitoring systems to determine gas composition in wide range of industrial process applications.

For combustion process control purposes, ECM delivers O2 and CO analysers. These analysers offer excellent precision and fast response and are suitable for virtually any flue gas composition and temperature.

For other process gas monitoring, ECM delivers rugged and accurate analysers designed for environment according to the requirement of the customer.

Most frequently monitored gases are:

O2

 N2O NH3 Cl Various hydrocarbons
NO SO2 CO HCl Hg
NO2 H2S CO2 HF H2

ECM is an experienced specialist in the field of particle content, flow and moisture monitoring in different gases. ECM offers a wide range of solutions for virtually all applications.

ECM manufactures a modular system of temperature controlled shelters to house monitoring and measurement systems. The shelters are equipped with air conditioning, instrument racks, cabling and other equipment according to the client specifications.

Measuring instrumentation is completed with elaborated data systems for transmission and processing of monitored values. The software is designed to reflect each client's requirements and meet national and local regulations.

Safety, comfort and health of people

ECM in cooperation with its partner organization TSI from USA offers a complete range of instruments for working environment quality control to enhance safety, comfort and health of people during their work in buildings, industrial working area, hospitals and rooms where dangerous gases or particulates are likely to occur.

This instrument technique includes:

  • monitoring and control instrument technique for indoors air quality
  • monitors for industrial hygiene and people safety
  • analytical equipment for diagnostics and research

Typical monitored parameters are the following:

  • air quality
  • air flow velocity
  • air distribution
  • pressure
  • temperature
  • humidity
  • solid particulates concentration
  • gases concentration

  • instrument technique for testing and research

Manufacture of isothermic shelters

ECM manufactures and delivers universal isothermic shelters for wide range of applications. Shelters are designed for installation of:

  • measuring instruments
  • gas and liquid analyzers
  • telecommunication equipment
  • navigation and hydrometeorological equipment, etc.

Aluminium sandwich construction provides for stability, excellent thermally-insulation characteristic and resistivity against corrosion. Shelters are supplied in stationary or mobile versions – mounted on separately towed trailers or van undercarriages. Dimensions and equipment of shelters are accommodated to customer requirements.
